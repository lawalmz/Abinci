What We can Offer

swallow

Swallow is a term coined for Nigerian solid meals that are taken with soups and ingested by “Swallowing”. They are naturally thick, elastic and easily malleable with a dough-like consistency once prepared

Rice Dishes

The thing about Jollof, fried rice etc...it’s that one dish across West Africa that is a unifying dish, Every country has their own version of rice dishes But the core is always two things—well, three things: rice, a tomato stew, and seasoning and all ingrediants

fruits

Among the important fruits are bananas, pineapples, dates, figs, olives, and citru. The banana is well distributed throughout tropical Africa, but it is intensively cultivated as an irrigated enterprise in Somalia, Uganda, Tanzania, Angola, and Madagascar.
